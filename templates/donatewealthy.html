DONATE FOR THE WEALTHY

As described in the Holy Quran, “al-Masjid al- Aqsa, whose surroundings We have blessed”, (Surah Al-Isra:1), and as the Messenger of Allah had said, “It is the second masjid decreed to be built by Allah”, (Bukhari and Muslim).

The virtuous days have begun and as a reminder of what the Prophet, peace, and blessings be upon him, said: (The days of righteous deeds are unparalleled in God's love). Hence, we launch with you DONATE FOR THE WEALTHY campaign that seeks to support and consolidate the steadfastness of Jerusalemites in their homes and land despite the pressures, harassment, and serious temptations they are facing.

Is it a dilapidated house in Jerusalem or a palace outside its walls? Despite offers of millions of dollars, they refused to give up their homes in Al-Quds city. Despite their steadfastness in the face of all attempts to displace them, Jerusalemites are constantly addressed in this way. Assist them in persevering despite temptations, rebuilding their homes, and supporting their steadfastness.

The Holy City's residents, however, are constrained in every aspect of their life and livelihood because plans are being hatched to force them to relocate.

There is either going out and getting rich and leaving their houses after being offered large sums of money OR staying in poverty.

Donating to this project will result in long-term benefits.

There are a number of projects included in the campaign which are :

Houses renovation in the old city project and supporting debtors, along with seasonal projects such as Eid al-Adha and sacrifices (Qurbani) in and outside of Al-Quds.

Regardless of whether you visit al-Quds, your deeds will bear witness to your presence!

You can donate as little as $1
